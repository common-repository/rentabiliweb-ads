<strong>English</strong>

Monetize your blog by offering your visitors/readers to buy advertising space on your blog.

<strong>Rentabiliweb Ads</strong> uses the service Micropayement <a href="http://www.rentabiliweb.com/en/">Rentabiliweb.com</a> to offer your visitors / readers buy advertising space on your blog so they can display their banner.

You can use one or more widget to display your sales areas or use the <strong>&lt;?php get_rentabads(ID'); ?&gt;</strong> plugin built to your sales space in your chart.

Create more spaces for sale in your blog with the option that own this extension and then add the advertising space :

<ul>
<li>in your widgets with the widget Rentabiliweb Ads</li>
<li>in your articles through the BBCode [rentabads]ID[/rentabads]</li>
<li>in your chart by using this feature <strong>&lt;?php get_rentabads('ID'); ?&gt;</strong></li>
</ul>
